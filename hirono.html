Hirono

Creator/Artist: Lang Studio

Theme: Exploring complex human feelings, life's ups and downs, hidden emotions, loneliness, joy, fear, and the journey of self-discovery.


With a curious mind and an adventurous spirit, Hirono spent days exploring the wonders of the forest, forming bonds with its magical creatures.Hirono wasn’t like the other villagers. While others followed the traditions of crafting and farming, Hirono was drawn to the ancient stories told by the elders. Hirono's melancholic, puffy-eyed look embodies hidden human emotions like sadness, fear, love, and resilience. His eyes reflects life's struggles and inner worlds through various series like "Reshape", "Shelter", and "Echo". Each figure tells a story about feeling lost, isolated, or finding hope.
