Skullpanda

Creator/Artist: Xiong Miao

Theme: Duality (life/death & light/dark), transformation, individuality, self-expression, and exploring different realities and identities.


This genderless character undergo transformative journeys through different realms, blending cute,spooky, and avant-garde styles with deep symbolism like life's duality, uncertainties, and self-expression. Each series tells new stories, from exploring ancient castles to mythical journeys, making skullpanda a collectible art toy that invites fans to explore the unknown and embrace individuality.


Each iconic series adds layer to the lore, with themes like:


Ancient Castle: An iconic series that launched Skullpanda to fame, blending fantasy and history.


Image of Reality:Explores deeper, symbolic meanings, hinting at Renaissance magic and gnomes.


Wonderland:A reimagining of classic tales, like Alice in Wonderland, with Skullpanda characters.


Love Story:Inspired by myths like Orpheus and Eurydice, focusing on journeys to find lost love.
