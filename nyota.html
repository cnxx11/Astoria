Nyota

Creator/Artist: Wang Ning

Theme: Slow living, discovering joy in the ordinary, cozy moments.


Born under autumn skies, Nyota is a curious girl who finds strenght in softeness, appreciating gestures, silence, and light. he enjoys unwinding in her cozy world, daydreaming with her cat Lumi, embodying the INFP personality type often associated with her. Nyota finds beauty in small things, and explores life's journey with quiet wonder, often depicted in various series like, "Growing Up", "I Am the Seasons", and "We Are All Stars , focusing on freindship, self-discovery, and mental wellness.


Her short story is one of finding magic in the everyday and embracing freedom and companionship.
